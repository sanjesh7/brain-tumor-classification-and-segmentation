About Tumors:

Brain tumors are abnormal growths of cells within brain tissue, categorized into benign (non-cancerous) and malignant (cancerous) types. These can originate in the brain (primary) or result from cancer spread (secondary). Common types include meningioma, glioma, pituitary tumors, and others.

Our website utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to accurately classify brain tumors. By analyzing JPEG images extracted from MRI slices, we aid in early detection.
